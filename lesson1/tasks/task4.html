Direct speech in oracular inscriptions

Select the part of the text that is a direct question.

貞丁卯其雨王占曰其雨
zhēn dīng mǎo qí yǔ wáng zhàn yuē qí yǔ
Which part is a direct question to the oracle?
貞丁卯
丁卯其雨
貞丁卯其雨
王占曰其雨

Explanation:

  1. 貞introduces a question - this is direct speech.
  2. 王占曰— the king interpreted, also direct speech, but this is an answer.

「貞丁卯其雨」 — full question: "Will it rain?"
